About me

Let me introduce myself

My name is Stefan Gogov, and I'm from Bulgaria. I live in Asenovgrad which is a small town in Plovdiv Province. I'm 18 years old, a 12-grade student at St. St Cyril and Metodiy Asenovgrad. Programming is my true passion and I cannot imagine life not doing it. I really enjoy solving complex problems and developing software solutions. I'm also a photography enthusiast and I try my best to capture anything beautiful.

My career so far

Always up for a challenge, I have worked for lean start-ups and was a member of the first New Zealand start-up to attend Y Combinator, the largest start-up accelerator in the world. From there, I worked my way up to Art Director and Team Lead at Appster where I oversaw the design of 30+ mobile and desktop apps. Currently, I lead UI/UX design at SaaS start-up VideoMyJob.
